Crystals Collector!

For each round, you will be provided a random number with four crystals below it.

Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden until discovered by clicking on it.

Each time a new round begins, each crystal is assigned a new value.

Win a round by matching your total score to the random number.

Lose the round if your total score goes above the random number.

Game is best out of 10 rounds
